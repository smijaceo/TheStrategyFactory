How It Works

Algorithmic precision.
Zero emotion.

Our scanner monitors NQ, Gold & Silver futures 24/5, identifying high-probability setups and executing with mechanical discipline.

Signal Detection

Proprietary algorithms scan for high-probability setups across multiple timeframes. Engineered filters ensure only the strongest signals trigger.

Directional Filter

A multi-timeframe directional filter ensures we only trade in the direction of the dominant trend. Every signal aligns with the higher-timeframe structure before entry.

Signals fire within seconds of detection. Entry, stop loss, and dual take-profit levels delivered straight to Discord with zero delay.
