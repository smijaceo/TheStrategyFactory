What is walk-forward testing?

Most trading systems are "backtested" — optimized and tested on the same historical data. That's like studying the answer key before the exam. Of course it looks good.

Walk-forward validation is different. We optimized our system on Year 1 data (the training set), then locked every parameter and ran it blind on Year 2 data it had never seen.

The result? Performance improved on unseen data across all three instruments. That's not luck — that's a robust system.
